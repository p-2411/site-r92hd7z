Thank you for lighting up my world the way you do.

For being so caring, so sweet, so kind, so... you. Your kindness makes my day when I need it most. You've shown me purity and beauty that I never thought was possible. Your hard work is admirable, and your dedication to the ones you love can't possibly be more attractive.

And how with every disagreement, every difficulty we face, we come out stronger, makes me feel something I'm not sure how to describe. I guess the best way I can describe it is.. when I see you, I see my future.

I see your adorable smile looming over me when we wake up in the morning. I hear your cute, audible gasps and giggles when we enter a 100m vicinity of any baby. I see a home that I come to, feeling safe and loved. I see a woman I'd die for, and most importantly, live for. Make a life for. I see a woman who pushes me to be the best person I can. And I see a family, our family.

Our beautiful children, raised by the most wonderful mum (you could say you learnt only from one of the best 😉). In a cozy house, by the coast. Not too big, nor too small. Just how you like it :) Maybe even our own dedicated chocolate fountain 😋

I thank your parents truly, for raising such an amazing girl. Happy 9 months mi amore, and I can't wait to celebrate many more to come ❤️.

From your love,
